Let's play some Matho.

Matho is like Bingo, but with math problems that you have to solve to win!

How to play:

  • Solve the problem.
  • Find the answer on your MATHO card and press it.
  • Call MATHO! When you have 5 answers pressed in a row, either horizontally, vertically, or diagonally.
